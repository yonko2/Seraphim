Seraphim – Emergency Assistant
Two parties: victim and helper/first responder
Automatic detection of physical and physiological accidents and alerting the emergency number 112.
• Transmitting information via an AI agent in the event of unconsciousness or other incapacitation of the victim.
• Filtering out human shock and panic from the transmitted information (providing clear, objective data).
• Integration with physical devices that measure health metrics – e.g., smartwatches, blood glucose monitors.
For the helper: The application uses a model to recognize the type of accident and provides the most appropriate actions for a bystander to take during the crucial minutes between the accident and the arrival of emergency services.
The emergency number 112 is alerted in this same manner.
